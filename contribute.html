Contribute

The COR-IPHES online osteological reference collection is designed to serve a broad audience, including the scientific community, educators, and the general public. By providing access to a comprehensive database of 3D models of skeletons and bones, the project aims to support research, education, and public engagement in the study of anatomy, archaeology, and paleontology. Our goal is to make this resource open and accessible to all, fostering learning and discovery across various fields.

As part of our long-term vision, this 3D database is intended to become a collaborative platform. We invite researchers, institutions, and individuals to contribute their own 3D models of skeletal remains or specific bones to help grow the collection. By adding new specimens, contributors will enrich the repository and enhance its value as a resource for comparative research and education.

To ensure the quality and consistency of the models added to the collection, a detailed contribution guide will be made available. This guide will provide clear standards and best practices for 3D scanning and model preparation, ensuring that each contribution meets the scientific and technical requirements necessary for integration into the COR-IPHES repository. Together, we can expand this valuable resource for the common good and support global collaboration in osteological research.
