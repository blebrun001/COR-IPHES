Welcome to the COR-IPHES
digital repository

An open-source digital repository

The COR-IPHES digital repository offers a collection of 3D models of mammal and bird bones from the IPHES osteological reference collection. Designed for comparative research in archaeology, paleontology, and zoology, this open-access database provides digitized specimens, facilitating the study and identification of animal remains in various scientific contexts.

Compliance to the FAIR principles

Findable

All the data are associated with Darwin Core metadata and Uberon ontology.

Accessible

DOI are provided for every data, making them really easy to cite. The data are stored in a Dataverse maintened by CORA-RDR.

Interoperable

All the files format used are standard and open sources (.OBJ, .GLTF etc.)

Reusable

The data are provided under CC-BY-4.0 licence
